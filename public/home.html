Calendar Project

This project allows users to keep track of events in their calendar as well as join groups with their own calendar.

General

This project was built with the MEAN Stack(MongoDB, ExpressJS, AngularJS, NodeJS)

How To Use

  • Sign in or create an account
  • Once in your homepage, you can add or edit your calender events
  • You must choose the date before adding, or else it will default to today's date
  • You can then join a group to share a calendar with other users
